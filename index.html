Brand Identity

Color Palette

Noir & Gold

Dramatic, confident, and opulent. A timeless combination for brands that command attention.

Champagne & Ivory

Light, serene, and exquisitely refined. Creates a clean and sophisticated atmosphere of pure elegance.

Deep Jewel Tones

Rich, royal, and exclusive. For brands that are established, confident, and deeply luxurious.

Typography

Classic Serif

The pairing is timeless and high fashion. The elegant heading font commands attention, while the clean body font ensures a comfortable reading experience.

Modern and Minimalist

Clean architectural, and contemporary. Using a single, versatile font family creates a sharp and cohesive look, perfect for a modern luxury brand that values precision.

Understated and Refined

Confident and intelligent. Using cohesive serif typeface for both headings and text creates a literary feel, suggesting a brand rooted in quiet, lasting quality.
